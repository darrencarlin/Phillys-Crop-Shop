About

Established in 2010. An alternative to conventional hairdressing, we do not sell products, it’s just us equipped with our trade skills and a clear conscience.

Ideal for those of you who are looking for a more personnel, professional and genuine approach, our service is as unique as you are.

Fully trained staff in both hairdressing and barbering. Our Stylist’s have each over twenty years experience. We bring with that a fresh approach and new ideas having worked for many years in salons in London, Brighton, Hove and Australia.

Salon
